Why This App Exists

Read Rise was built by Jake Maldonado — someone who grew up with a learning disability, who knows exactly what it feels like to fight through every single page while it looks effortless for everyone else. Nobody sees how much harder that life is. This app exists because that fight is real — and because it can be won.

Every feature in here — the guided lines, the drills, the quizzes, the ranks — came from lived experience, not theory. It was built to be the tool I needed and never had, made for me and for everyone who faced the same challenges I did.

And none of it happened alone. All glory to God, who turns struggles into purpose. The thing that was once my hardest battle became the thing I now get to use to lift other people up. That's not a coincidence — that's Him.

If reading has ever made you feel less-than: you're not. You never were.

KEEP MOVING — IT'S NEVER OVER.
— JAKE MALDONADO
